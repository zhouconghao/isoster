    4. Decisions

    • Decision: use explicit stop_code=2 in isoster for max-iteration fallback (same semantic bucket as photutils).
    • Rationale: prevents silent stop_code=0 mislabeling on non-converged rows and cleanly distinguishes convergence from max-iteration fallback.
    • Decision: treat stop_code=2 as acceptable in regular growth propagation.
    • Rationale: keeps behavior consistent with “valid but non-converged fallback geometry” use case and with photutils-style continuation.
